# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
- Teachers and Examiners who upload exam PDFs, run OCR parsing, structure questions, and manage student submissions.
- Students who take online exams under a clean, distraction-free layout.
- Annotators who consume downstream sequence-labeled data for training and validation.

## Product Purpose
To provide an online examination and OCR sequence-labeling system that converts PDF exam papers into structured interactive exams, allowing students to take tests and sending high-fidelity annotated data directly to labeling pipelines.

## Positioning
A high-fidelity OCR, token alignment, and examination platform designed around Notion-inspired clarity.

## Brand Personality
- Notion-inspired: Minimalist, quiet, focused, light-mode default.
- Precise: Accurate alignment of OCR data to source documents.
- Clean: Reduced cognitive overhead for test takers and graders.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, loud, or overdesigned SaaS dashboards with heavy nested cards, excessive animations, or decorative grid/diagonal backgrounds.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over Clutter:** Prioritize structural white space and crisp typography over decorative borders and shadows.
- **Accurate Token Mapping:** Keep OCR sequence labeling and PDF coordinate annotations visually precise and simple to edit.
- **Distraction-free Flow:** Clear away non-essential UI controls when students are active in an exam room.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clean typographic contrast hitting WCAG AA standards.
- Strong keyboard-navigation focus for interactive exam forms and question selections.
